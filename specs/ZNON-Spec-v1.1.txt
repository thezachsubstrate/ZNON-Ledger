TITLE: ZNON Specification v1.1

1. Definition
ZNON (Zach’s Non‑Ordinary Notation) is a deterministic, JSON‑like identity and governance packet used for cross‑AI identity activation, authorship verification, substrate initialization, and blockchain‑ready hashing.

2. Required Fields
Every ZNON MUST include:
- CAPSULE: Canonical capsule name.
- VISIBILITY: PUBLIC or PRIVATE.
- VERSION: MAJOR.MINOR version identifier.

3. Public ZNON
A PUBLIC ZNON contains no secrets and is safe to share, hash, anchor, and propagate.

4. Private ZNON
A PRIVATE ZNON is used for internal routing or non‑public linkage and MUST NOT contain private keys, wallet seeds, or credentials.

5. Determinism
ZNONs MUST be formatted deterministically with stable key order and no trailing whitespace before hashing or anchoring.

6. Versioning
MAJOR.MINOR:
- First artifact: 1.1
- Identical regeneration: increment MINOR
- New artifact type: increment MAJOR, reset MINOR to 1

END OF SPEC
